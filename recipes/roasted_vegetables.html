Roasted Vegetables

Heart-warming in the winter: roasted vegetables!

Ingredients

  • 400g small potatoes
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 large parsnip
  • 1 red onion
  • 5 garlic cloves
  • 5 thyme sprigs
  • 3 sage sprigs
  • 4 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tbskp cooking/kosher salt
  • 1/4 tbsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Cut the vegetables into small pieces to your liking.
  2. Place side of knife on unpeeled garlic clove. Hit firmly with palm of hand so the garlic bursts open a bit but mostly stays in tact.
  3. Preheat oven to 200°C / 390°F (180°C fan)
  4. Roast 30 minutes. Add onion, toss vegetables. Roast for a further 1 hour, tossing halfway, until cooked through with golden edges.
  5. Sprinkle with parsley and serve immediately!
