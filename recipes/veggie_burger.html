Veggie Burger

A lazy sunday feast: Burger!

Ingredients

  • Vegetables of your liking (e.g. tomato, lettuce)
  • 4 burger buns
  • 2 vegan burger patties of your choice
  • 3-4 tbsp vegan BBQ sauce

Steps

  1. Heat 1 tbsp of oil in a skillet or non-stick pan over medium heat.
  2. Cook the burger patties until they get brown and crispy on the outside.
  3. Brush half of the BBQ sauce onto the patties and let them caramelize.
  4. Assemble your burger patties with the vegetables of your choice.
  5. Add the hot burger patties and serve immediately.
